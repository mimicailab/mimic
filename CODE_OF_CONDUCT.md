# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Mimic is a professional open-source project. We expect all participants — maintainers, contributors, and community members — to treat each other with respect.

**Be kind.** Assume good intent. Give constructive feedback. Welcome newcomers. Disagree respectfully.

**Don't be a jerk.** No harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, trolling, or sustained negativity. No sexualised language or imagery. No doxxing or sharing private information.

This applies everywhere: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, Discord, social media, and any other space where you represent or interact with the Mimic community.

## Enforcement

Violations should be reported to conduct@mimic.dev. Reports are reviewed within 48 hours. All reports are confidential.

Consequences range from a private warning to a permanent ban, depending on severity and pattern. We prioritise the safety and comfort of the community over any individual's participation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Mimic community spaces and to any public space where an individual is representing the Mimic project or community.
